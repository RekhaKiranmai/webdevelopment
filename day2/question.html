FAQs

What is Virtual PPE?

Answer: Virtual PPE is a SaaS-based software solution that is designed to help companies move employees back into physical spaces safely and with reduced risk. It includes an administrative platform for managers and an app for employees. Virtual PPE helps companies understand governance requirements, securely collect data, assess employee actions and protect privacy in order to continuously protect employee health and manage corporate liability related to COVID-19.

Who is Virtual PPE design for?

Answer: Virtual PPE is designed for mid-to-large sized organizations with multiple employees working from a physical location such as general business, manufacturing, foodservice, hospitality, not-for-profit groups and more. Virtual PPE typically falls within the human resources, finance and risk management departments of the business.

How does Virtual PPE help re-open and operate a business safely

Answer: The Virtual PPE platform is deployed as an enterprise SaaS software application and manages employee inputs from the Virtual PPE app so managers have a record that can inform actions in the event that an employee is symptomatic or tests positive for COVID-19, or has been potentially exposed to someone meeting that criteria.

What is contact Tracking?

Answer:Contact tracing collects anonymized data on employee distancing and locations allowing employers to retrace activity and communicate critical health and safety information to others. Find out more on Contact Tracing at https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/php/principles-contact-tracing.html.

What are proximity alerts?

Answer:Employees receive a protective distancing alert via the app if they come close to a person who has indicated symptoms or has COVID-19. This helps individuals remain CDC compliant.
